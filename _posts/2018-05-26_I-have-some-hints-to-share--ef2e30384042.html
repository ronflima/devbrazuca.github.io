I have some hints to share. You can open your source code, but you don't need to open the entire code base. Open the components that are general, not domain specific. In fact, opening domain-specific code is not a good idea for several reasons and it does not have anything to do with trademarks or patents: they are so specific that will yield useless for other developers to reuse it.

General components, on the other hand, are not tied to a given domain and are reusable by nature. Opening it will increase its code quality and will create a community around it, giving some visibility to your business as a tech provider.
